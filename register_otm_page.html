One Time Mandate

Overview

One Time Mandate facility (OTM) facility is a one time mandate registraion process that will enable you to transact seemlesly as a simple, convenient and paperless manner. Using this OTM facility, you can instuct your bank to allow a debit of a certain amount, with a transactional upper limit in your amount, whenever you wish to transact. Having registed to OTM you can subscribe without having to initiate any payment from your end.

Benefit of the Facility

  • It is a one - time registration process.

  • Invest through any mode (Physical/SMS/Website) Via SIP or lump sum purchases.

  • Invest without a cheque/DD/transfer funds online.

  • Register SIP'S in 5 days for OTM regsiterd investors
